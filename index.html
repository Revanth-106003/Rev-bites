Kheer

  • 1/4 cup basmati rice
  • 4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoon chopped Dryfruits
  • 1 tablespoon Ghee

Wash and Soak Rice: Rinse the rice under running water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for about 30 minutes.
Cook the Rice: In a heavy-bottomed pot, heat the ghee over medium heat. Drain the rice and add it to the pot. Saut√© the rice for a couple of minutes until it's lightly toasted.
Add Milk: Pour in the milk and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer. Stir occasionally to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
Simmer: Let the rice cook in the milk for about 30-40 minutes or until the rice is completely cooked and the milk has thickened. Keep stirring occasionally.
Add Sugar and Flavorings: Add the sugar and stir until it dissolves completely. Mix in the cardamom powder, chopped nuts, raisins (if using), and saffron strands.
Simmer Again: Continue to simmer for another 5-10 minutes, stirring frequently, until the kheer reaches your desired consistency.
Serve: Remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with more nuts if desired.

Biriyani

  • 300 gms Basmati rice
  • 2 liters water
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt as required
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 bayleaf, cardamom
  • 1 inch cinnamon, 4 cloves
  • 600grms chicken
  • 1/2 tbsp turmeric
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp chili powder, ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatos
  • mint and coriander leaves

Cook the rice: Bring 2.5 liters of water to a boil. Add the basmati rice and a pinch of salt. Cook the rice until it's about 80% done, then drain and set aside.
Cook the chicken: Heat 2.5 tbsp of oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add the sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Add the ginger garlic paste and fry for a minute. Add the sliced tomatoes and green chilies, and cook until the tomatoes are soft.
Add spices and yogurt: Add the ready-made biryani masala, yogurt, chopped coriander leaves, and mint leaves. Cook until the chicken is tender and the gravy thickens.
Cook the rice with spices: In another pot, heat 2.5 tbsp of ghee. Add the green cardamom, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and caraway seeds. Add the partially cooked rice and cook until it's fully done.
Layer the biryani: In a large pot, layer half of the cooked rice at the bottom. Spread the chicken mixture over the rice. Add the remaining rice on top.
Cook on low heat: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for about 20-30 minutes.
Serve: Fluff the rice gently with a fork and serve hot with raita or a side salad.

Pasta

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Garlic
  • Lemon (zest and juice)
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Boil the Pasta: Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
Prepare the Sauce: In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and lemon zest, and saut√© until fragrant.
Add Cream and Lemon Juice: Pour in the heavy cream and lemon juice, stirring to combine. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.
Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
Season and Serve: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese before serving.

Salad

  • Mixed greens (arugula, spinach, and frisee)
  • Blue cheese
  • Toasted almonds
  • Shallots
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine vineger
  • Salt and Pepper

Prepare the Greens: Rinse the mixed greens thoroughly and dry them well. This ensures they are not soggy when dressed.
Make the Vinaigrette: In a small bowl, whisk together the red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced shallots, salt, and pepper. Slowly add olive oil while continuing to whisk until the dressing is emulsified.
Toast the Almonds: Lightly toast the almonds in a dry skillet over medium heat until they are golden and fragrant. Let them cool.
Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, crumbled blue cheese, and toasted almonds.
Dress the Salad: Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.
Serve: Plate the salad and enjoy!

Pizza

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, etc.)

Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
Add the Sauce: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
Add the Cheese and Toppings: Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add your desired toppings.
Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
Serve: Slice and serve hot.
